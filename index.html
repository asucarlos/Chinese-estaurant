Who We Are

With 29 locations across Ontario, our award-winning all-you-can-eat buffet offers over 100 delicious dishes showcasing the best in Chinese Canadian fare. Enjoy our extensive soup and salad bars, grill table, prime rib counter, sushi corner, and a variety of hot foods. An array of desserts, an ice cream sundae bar and frozen yogurt station offer the perfect end to the meal. Take-out and delivery, prepared fresh to order, are also available at every location.
